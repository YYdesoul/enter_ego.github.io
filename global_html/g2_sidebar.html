About Curator

Yiyun Li was born in 1994 in Anhui, China. Learned dance and painting since childhood. The undergraduate major is environmental art design, and the direction is exhibition design. Since 2019, she has been studying for a master's degree in Visual Art and Curatorial Studies at the New Academy of Fine Arts in Milan.
