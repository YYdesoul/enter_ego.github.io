Alter Ego as Emoji

Ego Emoji could present: Id, Individualism, Atman, Breath, Conscience, Al- ter ego, Self-interest, He, Psyche, Unconscious, Him, Alter, Anima, Mind, Number one, I, Other self, Preconscious, Self, Shade, Jiva, Soul, Heart, Spirit, Her, Personality, Pleasure principle, Shadow, She, Egotism, Pneuma, Libido, Inner man, Persona, Subconscious.

The online event is an integral part of the exhibition "Enter Ego’s Exit." The theme of the online event is "Alter Ego as Emoji." It provides a discussion space for the exhibition, where people can communicate and discuss with another identity. The curator guides the form of the event. It is divided into two parts. The first part is a simple online meditation. The purpose is to stim- ulate participants’ imagination of Ego in Emoji and find a position that fits their image in the online world. With the guidance of the curator, the visitors should adjust their comfortable sitting posture and relax by taking a deep breath. A few questions from the previous document will then be asked to make the visitors have a sense of the picture in their minds. And then, each of them needs to find one or more Emoji patterns that can represent them- selves. The second part is about the sharing of Emoji expressions combined with artworks. By using Emoji to describe the characters in the painting, the visitors can resonate with their own Emoji and imagine who they want to be if they are the person in the painting? Moreover, what kind of life they want to have? This activity aims to inspire people to find another ego in the art world by translating Ego into Emoji and then using the media of the online world to inspire people to learn empathy.
