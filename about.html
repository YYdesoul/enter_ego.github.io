About Curator

Introduction

Yiyun Li was born in 1994 in Anhui, China. Learned dance and painting since childhood. The undergraduate major is environmental art design, and the direction is exhibition design. Since 2019, she has been studying for a master's degree in Visual Art and Curatorial Studies at the New Academy of Fine Arts in Milan.

Timeline

My History

10.2019

The Shape of You

Yiyun initiated the art practice called "The shape of you" in October 2019. The artists and curators which come from different countries making dumplings together, not only to share memories of food, but also to shape another form of oneself. As the dumplings are boiled in the pot, everyone observes the changes in the shape of the dumplings and discusses with the theme of "Relational Aesthetics". This practice is a metaphor for the "be fused" of globalizationcultures, and the shaping of dumplings also reflects everyone's attitude towards their lives.

01.2020

Regarding the Pain of Others

Yiyun launched the art experiment called "Regarding the Pain of others" in January 2020 and invited curators and artists in NABA. At that time, the spark of covid-19 had just turned into a flame in China. There are no cases in Europe. Almost only Chinese people wear masks when they go out. The invited participants in this experiment project come from all over the world . They need to wear masks to enter and exit different public places on campus, such as canteens, libraries, lounges, toilets, etc., to experience this "uncomfortable feeling". Participants feel the pain of others through empathy, and the experience lasts about half an hour. After that, everyone sat together, writing or drawings on the mask. The purpose is to have in-depth exchanges with all mankind as a unified unit and oppose racial discrimination.
