About Exhibition

Enter Ego's Exit：
When Sitting Becomes a New Standing

The name of the exhibition "Enter Ego's Exit: When sitting becomes a new standing" implies that the current state of art is at a time node that may face changes. The first thing that needs to be challenged is our way of seeing the exhibition. This not only has an impact on the field of art, but also on human life style.

This online exhibition is a proposed project. In the case of limited space in a special period, it provides visitors with an experience of viewing the exhibition without leaving home. The exhibition is composed of three parts. The first part displays multimedia artworks created by artist Basim Magda, reflecting his thinking on the future and reflection on the past. The second part of the exhibition is based on the research of the artist Nam June Paik, combined with some documents.

The third part is an online meditation event organized by the curator. The audience can connect to the video in real time to achieve online interaction.
